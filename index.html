Here are my top 4s

Steins;Gate

Can't talk about anime without mentioning the best one, Steins;Gate. This anime have the best story telling surrounding sci-fi romance known to man.

Attack on Titan

Attack on Titan is one of the most mainstream anime on my list. Whether you choose to read the manga or watch the anime, you will not missed out on the thrill.

Vivy: Fluorite Eye's Song

If you're like me and loves sci-fi romance story, Vivy: Fluorite Eye's Song will fill your hunger to the brim. Not only that, the soundtrack in this anime is awesome.

5 Centimeters per Second

5 Centimeters per Second is my favorite Makoto Shinkai movie. Many would disagree, but this movie shows how reality is different from fairy tales, and how you need to embrace that fact.
